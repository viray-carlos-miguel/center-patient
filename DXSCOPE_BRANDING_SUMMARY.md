# DXscope - Elegant Medical Platform Branding

## 🎯 Brand Identity

**DXscope** represents the pinnacle of medical diagnosis technology - an elegant, sophisticated platform that combines cutting-edge AI with professional healthcare solutions.

### **Brand Name Meaning**
- **DX**: Medical/Diagnosis abbreviation
- **Scope**: Comprehensive analysis platform
- **Combined**: Advanced medical diagnosis scope
